D.Va, aka Hana Song, is a 19-year-old mech pilot, whose main role in Overwatch is a tank. She was formely a pro-gamer, having won many awards, but currently fights off the Omnix in her home town of Busan, South Korea. As part of the Mobile Exo-Force of the Korean Army, she is a hard worker, never letting her guard down, and a fierce fighter who shows no mercy to her opponents.

Besides fighting the Omnix and playing video games, D.va loves to stream her combat operations to her fans, eat unhealthy chips, and drink Nana Cola (which she even has a sponsorship for).

Her abilities consist of: Fusion Canons, Light Gun, Boosters, Defense Matrix, Micro Missiles, Self Destruct, and Call Mech.
